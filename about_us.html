About Us

Bright Future Prabidhik Shikshalaya (BFPS) is a modern educational institution in the Mid-West region of Lumbini Provience. This Institute is located in Dang District Ghorahi Sub-Metropolitan City 15 Bharatpur, Nepal. It was established in the year 2021 A.D to conduct technical, practical and short term training dedicated to meet the growing need for quality practical education. BFPS sprouted in the educational field with the support of academically conscious parents and the efforts of social service oriented people with the aim of providing quality technical and practical education wiht excellence and strict discipline has been established to provide with a team of committed and experienced trainer and dedicated teaching and non-teaching staff, the academy aims to make the institution a center of excellence by providing high quality academic services along with related academic activities.
